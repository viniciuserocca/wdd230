The Chamber of Commerce Site Plan

Site Name

Barretos Chamber of Commerce (Barretos is the name of the city which the chamber operates)

Site Purpose

  • Promote business and events around the city, so people who live here can know new places to go, and maybe find partners or sponsors.
  • Promote cultural landmarks in the city, so visitors can plan out places to visit while on vacation here.

Scenarios

  • What type of business will the chamber be promoting?
  • Will the chamber be promoting all of the events happening in the city?
  • Will the chamber promote information about politics?
